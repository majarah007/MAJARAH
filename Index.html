Washing Instructions
Care guide to preserve your garment's life
  • Hand Wash Cold Wash in cold water to keep fabric structured and soft.
  • Wash Inside Out Turn your piece inside out before washing to protect prints.
  • Do Not Tumble Dry Air dry naturally. Tumble drying will shrink or warp cotton.
  • Wash with Similar Colors Wash dark and light garments separately to avoid dye bleeding.
Garment Care
How to dry, iron, and store your pieces
  • Iron Low Heat Iron on a low setting. Never run the iron directly over printed graphics.
  • Do Not Bleach Bleaching agents destroy organic cotton fibers and ruin prints.
  • Dry in Shade Direct sunlight fades intense dyes. Dry indoors or in shaded areas.
  • Store Folded Hangers can stretch heavy cotton shoulder seams. Fold to store.
